About the App

The Taylor Family App is designed to help families stay connected through shared stories, family trees, and meaningful moments. It gives relatives a private place to celebrate history, relationships, and life together.

Core Features

Family Timeline

Capture life events, milestones, and memories in one shared space.

Relationship Mapping

Visualize family connections and build a living family tree.

Private Sharing

Keep family moments secure and visible only to the people who matter.

The Vision

This app is more than a project. It is a space where family history can be preserved, wisdom can be passed down, and future generations can understand where they come from.
